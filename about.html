About Me

Hi, I'm Harsh, and this is my brand. I'm passionate about growing my brand and much more. Here's a little more about me and my connection to this brand.

The name LeClo. This word has some hidden meaning inside it and I can break it for you below.
L : Stands for Luxury
E : Stands for Elegant
C : Stands for Comfort
L : Stands for Lifestyle
O : Stands for Original
At last I can say Leclo = "Luxury Elegance Comfort Lifestyle Originality."
This name makes it symbolic i.e. Leclo = "Light + Cloak" – Symbolizing clothing that brings light and positivity to people’s lives.
